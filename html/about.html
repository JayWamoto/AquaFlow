About AquaFlow

How AquaFlow Works

AquaFlow uses smart sensors to monitor water flow, detect leaks, and send live data to users.

1. Water Flow Monitoring

Smart sensors monitor water flow and detect leaks before they become serious.

2. Wireless Data Transfer

Water usage information is securely sent to the dashboard every few minutes.

3. Automatic Water Shutoff

If a major leak is detected, the system can automatically shut off the water supply.
