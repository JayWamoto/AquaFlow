The Engineering Behind AquaFlow

We use smart technology to track water usage, prevent water loss, and ensure efficient water delivery.

1.Water Flow Monitoring

Our smart sensors track water movement and spot hidden leaks by detecting small changes in how fast water flows.

2.Wireless Data Transmission

Water meter readings are safely sent to the cloud every 6 to 7 minutes, even when the meters are underground.

3.Smart Water Valves

If a pipe bursts, the system quickly turns off the water to stop flooding and damage.
